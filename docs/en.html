THE STUDIO

Arrange like the pros — without the bloat.

Drop an MP3 and it becomes a track. Split it into stems, cut clips at the playhead, move parts between tracks, set fades with a single grab.

  • Tracks with volume, pan, mute & solo — plus a slide-in mixer
  • Clip effects: reverb, echo, vocal cleanup, normalize, reverse
  • Full undo history with jump-to-any-state
  • Autosave & its own project format .audiola — everything in one file

SING-ALONG

Sing the lead — your version.

Audiola turns any song into your vocal booth: lyrics roll by, the note band shows the original melody, and you see live how well you hit it.

  • Punch-in: jump anywhere and re-sing from there
  • Count-in, latency calibration and mic picker with live level meter
  • "Make it perfect": auto-tune to the original melody — your voice stays yours
  • The take lands right in the studio as a new track

MASTERING

Loud, clean, done — on target.

Your mix runs through EQ, compressor and loudness normalization to exactly the LUFS you want. A/B switch for the honest comparison, waveform with click-to-seek.

  • 14 profiles: streaming, pop, rock, club, podcast … plus your own presets
  • BS.1770/EBU-R128 metering — the result hits the target loudness exactly
  • Per track too: "Master track" opens the same panel as a dialog
  • Export as WAV, MP3 or AAC — lyrics are written as tags & .lrc

EQUALIZER & SPATIAL

Shape your sound — in 2D and 3D.

The interactive 4-band EQ shapes the studio mix live — drag the dots, listen, done. And when stereo isn't enough: place every track freely in space.

  • Live preview right on the studio mix, curve with the real frequency response
  • Spatial: 7.1.4 bed, drag sources around the circle
  • Both are saved in the project and applied on export

VOICES

Your voice, as many as you like.

Create voice profiles from a short recording — locally on your GPU or via ElevenLabs. Swap a clip's voice, generate speech tracks from text.

  • Mic picker with live level — you instantly see the right one is connected
  • Local models (CUDA/CPU) with Whisper transcription
  • Only your own or licensed voices — fair is fair

LIGHT & DARK

Your studio, your light.

Night shift in the dark studio or daylight by the window — drag the handle.
